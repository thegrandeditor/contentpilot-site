ContentPilot - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

ContentPilot is an automated content publishing tool. This policy explains how we handle data.

Data Collection

ContentPilot does not collect, store, or share any personal user data. The app only accesses TikTok account data necessary to publish videos on behalf of the authenticated user.

Data Usage

Access tokens obtained through TikTok Login Kit are used solely to post videos via the Content Posting API. No data is shared with third parties.
